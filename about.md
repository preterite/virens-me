## Colophon {#colophon}

Built with [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/) and deployed on [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/).

### Typography

- **Headings**: [Cooper Hewitt](https://www.cooperhewitt.org/open-source-at-cooper-hewitt/cooper-hewitt-the-typeface-by-chester-jenkins/) (Smithsonian Design Museum, OFL)
- **Body**: [Libre Baskerville](https://github.com/impallari/Libre-Baskerville) (Impallari Type, OFL)
- **Code**: [JetBrains Mono](https://www.jetbrains.com/lp/mono/) (JetBrains, OFL)

All fonts are self-hosted as subsetted WOFF2 files (Latin Pro range). No external font services.
